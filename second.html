About Xtreative Market

Xtreative Market connects talented African artisans with global buyers. Our mission is to preserve traditional craftsmanship while supporting local communities in Rwanda and Uganda.

We work directly with cooperatives, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Every piece you purchase is a celebration of African heritage and creativity.

Our Story

Preserving African Heritage

Connecting Artisans With Global Markets

Xtreative Market was founded in 2020 with a mission to empower talented African artisans by providing them with access to global markets while preserving traditional craftsmanship.

We work directly with cooperatives and individual artisans across Rwanda and Uganda, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Each product in our collection tells a unique story of cultural heritage and skilled craftsmanship.

By purchasing from Xtreative Market, you're not just acquiring a beautiful handmade item - you're supporting livelihoods, preserving traditions, and celebrating African creativity.
